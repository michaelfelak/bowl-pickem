Presented by Lookaway Inn

Welcome to 2024-2025 Bowl Season!

This year introduces the 12 team college football playoff. As a quick introduction to the new format, you will still select bowls as normal. The first round of the playoffs AND the quarterfinals are included. In the quarterfinals matchups, you will see that you can pick the top seeds -or- either of the bottom 2 seeds. This means that if you pick the lower seeds, you will be awarded points if either of those teams advance and win. You will also pick the championship game and the national champion. Each of the championship game picks will be worth 2 points if correct. The champion will be worth 5 points. Neither will deduct points from your total score. None of your picks have to coordinate. For example, you could pick Team A and Team B to be in the championship game, but pick Team C to win the championship.
