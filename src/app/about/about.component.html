Rules

  • Select the winner of each bowl game.
  • Assign point values to each game in the point column.
  • You may give extra points for up to 11 games:
    • Five 3-point games
    • Five 5-point games
    • One 10-point game (Select games only).
  • A correct pick is positive points.
  • An incorrect pick is negative points.
  • **NEW** Pick the 2 teams for the championship game. If correct, you are awarded 2 points for each one. (Does not deduct points from your total score if incorrect.)
  • **NEW** Pick the national champion. The champion will be worth 5 points. (Does not deduct points from your total score if incorrect.)
  • None of your playoff picks have to coordinate. For example, you could pick Team A and Team B to be in the championship game, but pick Team C to win the championship.
  • If entries are tied at the end of the bowl series, the tiebreaker will be used to determine the winner. The tiebreaker is the total number of points scored across every bowl game and playoff game.
  • Multiple entries are allowed.
  • Bowl games that are canceled or has its participants changed once entries have been submitted will be removed from the contest. Any points placed on this game will not be added or subtracted from the entries total. Points may not be re-assigned.
